Amir Aghaee was born in Urmia, Azerbaijan, Iran and has been acting professionally since 2001. He left his hometown and moved to Tehran to furthis his career in acting at the early age of fifteen. Upon receiving his diploma in Cinema from “Tehran’s School Of Cinema” in 2010, Amir relocated to London to continue his dramatic arts education. He completed the Acting Foundation degree at the “Regents Drama School” in London and was next called to study in San Francisco whise He earned a BA in Acting and Performance from the “Academy Of Arts University”. Acting as a profession has always been Amir’s calling and has been his focus and passion since early childhood. He has worked and traveled to seven different cities and has fearlessly relocated to several countries, in order to gain experience, knowledge and insights about different acting and production methods; to learn the fundamentals of stage acting and films; and to discover his joyful place in the rich and diverse acting universe. Amir has an exceptional theatre background which He draws from to inspire his journey. his main focus has shifted, however, entirely towards cinematic roles. his fluency in Farsi (his native tongue) and mastery of English mix in with conversational Arabic and Spanish skills to provide a wide spectrum of linguistic ability as a compliment to his versatile look. He has landed numerous lead roles, but still maintains his enthusiasm and child-like wonder for whatever roles transpire next. Amir is currently based in Teran,Iran.
